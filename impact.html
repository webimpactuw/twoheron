Wolf Hollow Wildlife Rehab is a non profit organization that promotes the well-being of wildlife and their habitats through rehabilitation of injured and orphaned wildlife, public education and non-invasive research. They care for injured, orphaned, or sick native wild animals and release them back into the wild and educate other organizations. Their non-invasive research focuses on gathering information that will help improve standards of animal care and increase post release survival rates of rehabilitated animals.

Wolf Hollow Wildlife Rehab

/dev /color is the global career accelerator for Black software engineers, technologists, and executives. They have grown to a community of more than six hundred professional members with nearly a thousand leaders having participated for one or more years in A* (A Star), their flagship peer mentorship program for software engineers. /dev/color brings Black engineering leaders together to surface and solve some of their most pressing challenges.

/dev/color

Geeking Out Kids of Color is an organization that builds and sustains youth programs that are rooted in community, computer science, and consciousness. Their project-based tech curricula are informed by anti-racism, anti-sexism, multiculturalism, social-emotional learning, and decolonization frameworks. Their programs are geared towards empowering youth with the voice, digital literacy, and problem-solving skills to envision and construct new realities.

Geeking out Kids of Color

The National Resources Defense Council works to ensure the rights of all people to the air, the water and the wild, and to prevent special interests from undermining public interests. They use data and science to blueprint transformative solutions, and mobilize the support of partners, members, and activists to advocate for laws and policies that will protect the environment far into the future.

Natural Resources Defense Council

Climate Solutions is a Northwest-based clean energy economy nonprofit that works to champion transformational policies and market-based innovations; catalyze powerful partnerships and a diverse movement for action and accountability; and communicate a bold vision for solutions at the scale required by climate science. To drive public policy change faster and to foster more private innovation, we primarily work to pass and implement policies at the state level and in major jurisdictions using the tools of advocacy, research, communications and organizing outreach.
